About Ekduo Ltd

Leading the way in sustainable agriculture and agro-processing

Our Agricultural Excellence

Ekduo Ltd specializes in both primary agriculture and agro-processing. We are engaged in soybeans farming and the processing of oil palm fruits into various high-quality products and vital by-products.

Our production includes crude palm oil, crude palm kernel oil, palm kernel cake, and palm kernel shells. Additionally, we trade potassium carbonate (potash), a fundamental substance for the soap manufacturing industry.

Our Unique Selling Proposition

Superb customer satisfaction services with a prompt complaint resolution system that builds high confidence in our business relationships.

Our Product Portfolio

High-quality agricultural products and by-products for diverse industries

Oil Products

Crude Palm Oil (CPO)

Directly processed from palm fruits for food consumption, vegetable oil refining, and soap production. Our CPO meets the highest quality standards for industrial and food applications.

Key Applications

Food Production Soap Making Vegetable Oil
Oil Products

Crude Palm Kernel Oil (CPKO)

Produced from palm nuts, ideal for vegetable oil refining, soap, and cosmetics production. This versatile oil is a key ingredient in many industrial applications.

Key Applications

Cosmetics Soap Production Cooking Oil
Agricultural Products

Soybeans

Protein-rich leguminous crop for direct consumption and processing into various food products. This is a leguminous crop rich in protein and oil. It is a highly consumed staple food both locally and international.

Key Applications

Human Consumption Animal Feed Food Processing
By-Products

Palm Kernel Cake (PKC)

A vital by-product of producing CPKO, rich in roughage and oil for animal feed. An economical and nutritious feed ingredient for livestock and poultry.

Key Applications

Livestock Feed Poultry Feed Animal Nutrition
Industrial Products

Potassium Carbonate (Potash)

Essential base for soap making, especially medicinal African black soap. We provide high-quality potash for traditional and industrial soap production.

Key Applications

Soap Making Chemical Industry Traditional Soap
By-Products

Palm Kernel Shells

Valuable biomass material used as renewable energy source. Ideal for charcoal briquettes production and as boiler fuel in industrial applications.

Key Applications

Briquettes Renewable Energy Boiler Fuel
